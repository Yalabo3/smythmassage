Conditions We Treat

Massage therapists are soft tissue specialists who focus their treatment on muscles, tendons, ligaments, joint capsules and fascia. Massage therapy is beneficial for the treatment and maintenance of many conditions, including:

Headaches
Whiplash
Tendonitis
Pre-natal and post natal
Sprains, strains, muscle spasms
Arthritis
Carpal Tunnel Syndrome
Fibromyalgia
Stress management

Scheduling

We now have two convenient ways to schedule your massage session. Use our secure online scheduling system. If you can't find a suitable time or it's an emergency, please call us at (613) 260-2704 and we will return your call as soon as possible.

Cancellation Policy

We require a 24-Hour Notice for appointment cancellations. If you cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, you will be charged 50% of the scheduled fee. This does not apply to same-day appointments. If you must cancel due to illness or an emergency, please call as soon as possible.

No Show Policy

If you do not show for your appointment, you will be charged 100% of the scheduled fee.
